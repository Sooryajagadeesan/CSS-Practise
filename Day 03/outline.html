CSS Outlines

Outlines are lines that are drwan outside of the border of an element.

NOTE : Eventhough the borders are not set, the outline will take its presence surrounding the element, This is still an ouline but it may look like a border. Important point is that outlines don't take part in the content area but they overwrite upon the sibling elements. To avoid the overwriting we can use margins...!
